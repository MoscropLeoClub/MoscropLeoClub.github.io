Our History

In 1957, the first ever Leo club was founded by Glenside Lions Club in Abington, Pennsylvania, USA with only 35 high school boys. Through decades of hard-work and dedication, as of January 2017, the Leo Club has grown to become an extensive international youth program with more than 15,000 members in 139 countries. It provides youth of the world with an opportunity for development and contribution, individually and collectively, as responsible members of the local, national and international community. Pursuing the club motto, the Moscrop Leo Club takes part in various service projects and collaborates with other organizations. We are all about service to the community, to making a difference with small steps and working together towards a better future. We are about commitment, respect, and dedication.

For more information, visit lionsclubs.org
